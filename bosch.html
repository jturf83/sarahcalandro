DATA-MINING, THE BOSCH GROUP / 2011

The Data-Mining team at The Bosch Group invited me to Palo Alto, CA to create an internal video for their team. The goal was to communicate to company executives what the data-mining team does, how it works, and the vision for data-mining in the future. I spent three weeks learning about data-mining and the team, crafting a story that makes data-mining easy to understand, and animating a video to bring this story to life.
