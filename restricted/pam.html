PEOPLE & MONEY, CAPITAL ONE / 2014

After researching and designing Living Money, I was asked to give a talk about it at PAM, Capital One’s bi-annual People and Money conference. My talk covers what Cruise Control is, the painful barriers today that keep people from knowing if they’re okay, and Capital One’s role in scaling beyond the checking account: to Cruise Control 2.0.
